Why SangamX

Despite annual global contributions of $2.5 Trillion on social organizations, despite having nearly 10% of the private sector workforce deployed in the non-profit sector, we have not materially moved the needle on it’s social impact

The income gap between richest-poorest household is largest in 50 years. About 10% of world population still lives in extreme poverty today. Almost 75% of earth's surface has been altered threatning 1 million animal species to extinction

We are living in a world where the traditional boundaries between the three sectors are no longer distinct. A for-benefit economy or fourth sector is emerging where these organizations do not depend only on philanthropy, and generate a substantial proportion of their resources from commercial activities.

We believe that incorporating the good of free-market mechanisms and creating a similar ecosystem appropriate for NPOs and Social Enterprises, will multiply the delivered impact

SangamX Services

In line with this concept, and our vision, SangamX is created  as a platform that brings together a collection of capabilities needed to bring that free-market like effectiveness to the entire social impact sector – a markets mechanism we are calling Societal Markets.. a la Capital Markets, which will help magnify the impact per dollar per societal project.

Working in tandem with a Social Stock Exchange, our Societal Markets Framework creates a collection of capabilities needed to bring the societal free markets mechanism to life – some of these will be owned and operated by SangamX, while some will be owned/ operated by partners. We will leverage and connect existing mechanisms such as Social Stock Exchanges, match-making platforms, crowd-funding platforms etc. In doing so, we will role model a socially responsible AND financially sustainable organization, which establishes that financial return is only a means to create social returns, never the other way.
